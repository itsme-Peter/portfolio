HackTheBox | GreenHorn

In this walkthrough, I demonstrate how I obtained complete ownership of GreenHorn on HackTheBox
0xBEN
Jul 23, 2024 9 min read

Initial Foothold Hint

  • Do your due diligence with the enumeration. You should find a password hash that cracks very easily on one of the services, which will lend a hand in execution of a CVE on the target.
  • You should be able to find a public exploit for the CVE. Or if you're especially motivated, it's not hard to manually create a payload that will work for this exploit.

Privilege Escalation Hint

  • Lateral Pivot to Local User
    • Always look for the easy wins with any information already in your possession
  • Becoming Root
    • Do some research on how you can extract and deobfusacate some information found during your post-exploit enumeration
ℹ️
For additional hints and assistance, chat with your peers in the HackTheBox Discord server

Don't miss an opportunity to find some breadcrumbs in the nmap output. We can see the HTTP redirect to greenhorn.htb and should add this to our /etc/hosts file.
echo -e '10.129.191.235\t\tgreenhorn.htb' | sudo tee -a /etc/hosts
